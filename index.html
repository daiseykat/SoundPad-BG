SoundPad

asdf

Modern day song writers often write lyrics over beats in mp3 format. When writing lyrics you need the ability to jump around the track so you can work on separate sections, like the chorus, verse 1, outro, etc. You need the ability to jot down ideas, both as written lyrics and as recorded rough takes. The beatmaker and the song writer need the ability to collaborate. This is how songs are written. It's a process.

SoundPad would integrate the process of listening to SoundCloud files, segmenting and looping audio, writing lyrics, recording rough takes, sharing private files, and creating interaction between the song writer and the music producers - in one app.

Soundcloud

Once logged in, you can access soundcloud files and users. Files can be shared privately.

Playback

You can play, loop and restart the track. Buttons should be big enough to easily/quickly manage start points so you can stay "in the flow" of writing lyrics.

Loops

You can add end/stop points to create a loop. This way, you can practice one section at a time on repeat.

Lyrics

You can write your lyrics in the notepad. Lyrics can be saved, shared and commented on by other users.

Practice Mode

You can play the track with "in-place comments" to show key words in the lyrics, such as the first few words of the next verse.

Record

You can record yourself singing over the track, so you can save and share rough ideas.

Share

You can share your files with other users, allowing them to leave in-place comments and feedback (similar to how Soundcloud does their comments)
